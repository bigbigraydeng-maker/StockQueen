Meet the co-founders behind StockQueen — two entrepreneurs bridging technology, finance, and AI from the heart of Australia and New Zealand.

Richard Hu

Co-Founder

Investor | AI Applications | Equity Strategy

Brisbane / Auckland

About

Richard Hu is an investor and entrepreneur with over 14 years of professional experience across New Zealand and Australia. Based between Brisbane and Auckland, he has built deep expertise in residential property markets, client consultation, and high-value commercial sales operations across both countries.

Investment & Finance

Richard is an active participant in the investment space, with interests in U.S. equities, quantitative trading systems, and early-stage ventures. He focuses on identifying data-driven opportunities and applying systematic approaches to portfolio management, with particular interest in momentum-based strategies and quantitative research.

Technology & AI

In recent years, Richard has expanded his focus into technology-driven business models and artificial intelligence applications. He actively applies AI tools to improve research, analysis, and decision-making across multiple domains including real estate evaluation, financial markets, and digital business operations.

Strategic Thinking

Richard brings a disciplined, probability-based approach to decision-making — honed through semi-professional Texas Hold'em poker, where he applies game theory, expected value frameworks, and bankroll management. This analytical mindset directly complements his investment strategy and risk management philosophy.

Ray Deng（大瑞）

Co-Founder

AI Strategy | Cross-Border Operations | Fintech

Auckland

About

Ray Deng is an Auckland-based cross-border entrepreneur and AI strategist. He is the founder of MoveHub, Compass AI, and WorkVisas.work, building innovative solutions that connect China, New Zealand, and Australia. With deep local expertise in the Australia and New Zealand market, Ray combines strategic vision with hands-on execution.

AI & Technology

Ray focuses on developing and implementing AI strategies that transform business operations. From machine learning models to natural language processing, he creates intelligent systems that enhance decision-making and automate complex workflows. His technical depth spans AI system architecture, quantitative modelling, and fintech infrastructure.

Cross-Border Finance & Commerce

Ray builds bridges between Chinese, Australian and New Zealand markets, identifying opportunities for trade, investment, and collaboration while navigating regulatory frameworks and cultural nuances. He works with founders, investors, and cross-border operators to turn complex ideas into executable ventures.

Workforce & Mobility Solutions

In the labour mobility space, Ray creates platforms that connect talent with opportunities across borders, simplifying visa processes, job matching, and workforce integration for international professionals moving between Asia, Australia and New Zealand.

Our Shared Vision

Based across Australia and New Zealand, Richard and Ray share a conviction that AI-driven quantitative strategies can democratise access to institutional-grade investment tools. StockQueen was born from the intersection of their complementary strengths — Richard's investment acumen and market insight, combined with Ray's AI engineering and cross-border operational expertise.
