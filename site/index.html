Origin of the Name

StockQueen started as an internal codename.

In the early days of strategy development, we needed a core module to:

  • ◆ Monitor the entire stock universe
  • ◆ Score every ticker
  • ◆ Decide where capital should flow

This module was the "brain" of the entire system.

"The team named it Queen for a simple reason."

In chess, the Queen is the only piece that can move:

📊
Time
🎯
Market
⚡
Factor

Our algorithm moves across multiple dimensions — time, market, factor, and risk — constantly rearranging the entire portfolio.

The internal codename became the system's identity: StockQueen

Strategy Overview

Multi-Factor Scoring

Proprietary scoring engine evaluates 80+ tickers across momentum, fundamentals, and market structure dimensions.

Regime Adaptive

Automatically detects bull, bear, and choppy market phases. Dynamically rotates between offensive, defensive, and inverse positions.

Risk Managed

ATR-based stop-loss and take-profit on every position. Sector concentration limits and portfolio-level drawdown controls.

Walk-Forward Validated

Rolling 3-month training windows with monthly out-of-sample testing. No lookahead bias — every backtest decision uses only historically available data.
