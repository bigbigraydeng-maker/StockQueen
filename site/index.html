Strategy Overview

Multi-Factor Scoring

Proprietary scoring engine evaluates a dynamic 500-ticker universe across momentum, fundamentals, and market structure dimensions. Pool refreshed quarterly with liquidity filters.

Regime Adaptive

Automatically detects bull, bear, and choppy market phases. Dynamically rotates between offensive, defensive, and inverse positions.

Risk Managed

ATR-based stop-loss and take-profit on every position. Sector concentration limits and portfolio-level drawdown controls.

ML Walk-Forward Optimization

Each month, the model evaluates 25 parameter combinations and selects the best-Sharpe config on trailing 3-month data — then tests forward on unseen data. 40 rolling windows, zero lookahead.

Methodology & Bias Controls

  • Validation: 40-Window Walk-Forward OOS (3mo train + 1mo test, 25 param combos) — not a simple backtest
  • Universe: Dynamic 500-ticker pool (large-cap + mid/small-cap growth + sector ETFs + defensive + inverse)
  • Holdings: Top-N adaptive (2–6), weekly rotation
  • Bias Controls: 0.1% slippage per side · 500K daily volume filter · next-day open execution (no close-price look-ahead)
